# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

- Be direct and specific in issues and PRs — no vague requests
- Critique code, not people
- Accept that maintainers may close issues or PRs without explanation
- No spam, self-promotion, or off-topic discussion

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks of any kind
- Submitting issues purely to promote other projects
- Opening duplicate issues without checking existing ones first

## Enforcement

Violations result in immediate removal from the project without warning.
